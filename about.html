About History Journal

History Journal is a digital archive showcasing the work of The Eastern Washington University history club. Our mission is to provide a platform for in-depth research, student submitted papers, and academic discussion in history. We welcome submissions from students and contributions from faculty.

The journal is curated by an editorial team dedicated to maintaining academic excellence and promoting historical discourse.
